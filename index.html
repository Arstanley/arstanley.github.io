Bio

Welcome! I am a PhD student at Vanderbilt University, working with Professor Tyler Derr. I graduated from the University of Notre Dame with an honor Bachelor degree in 2021, where I worked at the Notre Dame DM2 Lab with Prof. Meng Jiang on data mining and anomaly detection. In the past, I have been fortunate to work with Prof. Dong Wang in the Notre Dame Social Sensing Lab. Before starting my PhD, I was a software development engineer at Amazon Web Services.
